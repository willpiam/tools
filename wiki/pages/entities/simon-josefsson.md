# Simon Josefsson

## Summary
- Author of RFC 4648, which defines interoperable Base16/Base32/Base64 encoding behavior.
- Published under IETF Standards Track context in October 2006.

## Key details
- RFC 4648 credits Simon Josefsson (SJD) as author and marks the memo as obsoleting RFC 3548.
- The document also references an ISO C99 Base64 implementation hosted by the author as non-normative supporting material.
